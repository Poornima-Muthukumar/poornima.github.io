My Story

I grew up in India before I moved to Singapore where I did my University. Singapore is a superbly organized place. I learnt a great deal in my four years in Singapore about life, work and computers. I started my first job with Goldman Sachs as a Software Developer. I worked there for a few months until I decided to shift to Microsoft. I moved to Dublin, Ireland in Jan 2015 and worked there with Mircosoft for a year and a half. Dublin is the most fun place I have ever lived in. It gave me to opportunity to work and become friends with diverse people from all over Europe. I moved to Seattle in August 2016 and have been working here since then.
